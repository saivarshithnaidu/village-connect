VILLAGECONNECT - PROJECT REVIEW & ARCHITECTURE DOCUMENT

Generated on: 2026-04-06

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
-------------------
VillageConnect is a high-fidelity, production-grade community platform designed to bridge the gap between rural citizens and resolution providers (volunteers/admins). It empowers villagers to report localized issues (infrastructure, water, roads, etc.) using AI-assisted reporting tools and multi-lingual support.

2. CORE FEATURES
----------------
*   Smart Reporting: AI-powered issue analysis using Gemini Vision to auto-generate titles, descriptions, and severity levels from uploaded photos.
*   Visual Submission: Integrated professional Live Camera and multi-image gallery support for providing clear evidence.
*   Localized Experience: Full end-to-end support for English and Telugu (via next-intl), including AI-powered on-the-fly translation of issue descriptions.
*   Intelligent Registry: A cascading location selection system (District > Mandal > Village) with a "Manual Fallback" mechanism for unregistered locations.
*   Real-time Infrastructure: Powered by Supabase Realtime for instant updates on issue status, upvotes, and community comments.
*   Actionable Engagement: Role-based workflows for Villagers (report), Volunteers (claim/resolve), and Admins (verify/moderate).
*   Outcome Intelligence: Automated AI resolution synthesis that generates professional "Case Reports" once a problem is closed.
