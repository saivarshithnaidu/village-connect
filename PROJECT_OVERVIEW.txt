================================================================================
                           VILLAGE CONNECT - PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

VillageConnect is a high-impact digital platform designed to bridge the gap 
between rural citizens (Villagers) and community leaders (Admins/Volunteers). 
The goal is to provide a transparent, efficient way to report and resolve local 
infrastructure and social issues.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE CORE PROBLEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In many villages, problems like broken water pipes, poor road conditions, or 
electricity failures go unreported for weeks because there is no direct channel 
to the authorities. VillageConnect solves this by putting the power of reporting 
in every villager's pocket.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. KEY ROLES (WHO IS WHO?)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. THE VILLAGER (Reporter)
   - Roles: Report new problems, track the status of their reports, and upvote 
            existing issues to show community priority.
   - Purpose: Be the "eyes" of the village.

B. THE VOLUNTEER (Worker/Problem Solver)
   - Roles: View reported problems, propose technical or manual solutions, and 
            update the progress of assigned tasks.
   - Purpose: Perform the physical work needed to fix the community.

C. THE ADMIN (Authority/Panchayat)
   - Roles: Full dashboard access. Verify if reports are legitimate, assign 
            specific workers to tasks, and monitor village-wide statistics 
            (completion rates, category distribution).
   - Purpose: Direct and oversee the entire community improvement process.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. DATA FLOW (HOW IT WORKS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. REPORT: A Villager posts an issue (e.g., "Water Leak in Sector 4").
2. PRIORITY: Other villagers upvote the issue, moving it to the top of the list.
3. VERIFY: An Admin verifies the report to ensure it's not spam.
4. PROPOSE: A Volunteer proposes a fix (e.g., "Replacing the main PVC pipe").
5. ASSIGN: The Admin assigns the Volunteer to the task.
6. RESOLVE: Once fixed, the status is updated to "Resolved", and the Villager 
   is notified of the success.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. TECHNOLOGY STACK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- FRONTEND: Next.js 15 (Modern, Fast, Server-Side Rendering)
- STYLING: Tailwind CSS 4 (Premium Glassmorphism & Modern UI)
- DATABASE: Supabase (PostreSQL for data, Auth for secure login)
- BACKEND: Next.js API Routes (Serverless architecture)
